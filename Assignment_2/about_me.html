Hey! Its a little about me!! "Oswald"

Have a great day!! -- Oswald & Weenie

Oswald's character is a thoughtful blue octopus named Oswald who lives in an apartment complex. Oswald is an octopus that loves to have fun and is cautious about safety. He and his dog, Weenie, reside in an apartment building in Big City. His neighborhood is full of colorful characters, such as Madame Butterfly, the owner of the Big Diner, and her daughter, Catrina Caterpillar, as well as his friend Henry the Penguin. Oswald enjoys singing and playing the piano, and he always goes above and beyond to support his neighbors and friends.
